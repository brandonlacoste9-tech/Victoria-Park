# RendezVous AI — Pricing Draft

All prices in **CAD**, billed monthly. Annual billing = **17% off** (two months free).

| Tier | Price | Locations | Staff | Bookings/mo | SMS | Voice minutes | Key features |
|------|-------|-----------|-------|-------------|-----|---------------|--------------|
| **Starter** | $49 | 1 | 1 | 100 | 200 | 100 | Web widget, calendar sync, basic dashboard |
| **Pro** | $149 | 1 | up to 5 | Unlimited | 1,000 | 500 | SMS automation, lead pipeline, recovery analytics, multi-staff |
| **Premium** | $349 | up to 5 | Unlimited | Unlimited | 5,000 | 2,500 | Multi-location, advanced voice flows, white-label, priority support, custom prompts |

## Overage (Starter only)

| Resource | Rate |
|----------|------|
| Extra booking | $0.50 |
| Extra SMS | $0.10 |
| Extra voice minute | $0.15 |

## Free trial mechanics

- 14 days, **no credit card** required to start
- Day 12 email + SMS nudge: *"Trial ends in 2 days — add payment to keep your recovered revenue rolling"*
- If card added, full **Pro tier** for the trial (better conversion data)
